Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2025

This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect personal data when you use the TikTok Ads Data App.

What We Collect

  • Your TikTok account ID (with your consent)
  • Ad campaign performance data accessible via the TikTok API
  • Basic OAuth authentication information (such as access tokens)

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data solely for generating performance reports and visualising TikTok Ads metrics for your own use.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Data is accessed only by you and stored in your own connected systems.

Data Storage & Security

  • We do not store personal data or analytics on our servers.
  • All access tokens are stored securely and encrypted.
  • You can revoke access at any time via your Pinterest account settings.

Legal Basis (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we process your data only with your explicit consent. You may withdraw consent at any time.

Your Rights

  • Right to access and review your data
  • Right to request correction or deletion of your data
  • Right to withdraw consent and revoke access
